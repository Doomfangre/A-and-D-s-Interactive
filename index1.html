Hymn of Daedalus

Start Journey

Up above the clouds, there is a kingdom in which you play as our main protagonist. Sora, the prince and heir to the king. You reside in the living castle along with your mother and younger sister.

However, you notice a change in your sister's behavior but don't think much of it. Tragically, only a week later, the parents, who are the king and queen, were found deceased due to an unknown cause.

In the kings will, you were supposed to become the new ruler of the kingdom. However, the head royal guard declared your sister to be the ruler of the kingdom.

The head guard explained to the public how the deaths of the king, queen, and the heir to the throne were tragic. He explained that the bloodline will live through the soon to be queen of the living castle.

Confused, he confronts his sister, however his sister is not the same as she was before her disappearance. She had become significantly stronger and when you confronted her, she had a weapon in her hand.

You were beaten almost to the brink of death, and was slowly losing consciousness as you were dragged to the edge of the clouds. She explains how she was the one who ended their parents and how now she will end you.

She then kicks you off the edge of the clouds. You lose consciousness from the power kick and rapidly fall down to the earth, unknowingly starting your journey.

The Spider Den

Feeling the warmth of the sun in your face, you awake still in pain from the wounds your sister gave you. You find yourself in a giant spider web inside an open area with rocky walls and a giant opening at the top.

You spot the light hue of the blue spider wandering in the dark, staring at you. Panicked, you try to escape the web but are unable to.

You decide to wriggle out, however you werent able to.

After constant wriggling you make it out of the web. You catch your fall and decide to escape so you enter a nearby cave entrance in the spider's den

The cave is too small for the spider to pursue. You wander in the darkness, limping while hugging hard cold walls of the cave.

You suddenly feel something small brush past your leg. Shaken up at first, you halt trying to detect the unknown creature.

However, you hear its pitter patter footsteps running the same direction as you were going. Relived, you follow it until eventually you make it out of the cave.

You exit the cave and the unknown creature was actually small and fuzzy. You thank the unknown creature for its support and continue on your way.

You look at your surroundings and you find a nearby rusty hand axe and a frail looking sword.

You grab the axe and cut the web, you drop the axe trying to catch your fall, the axe falls on the ground but it is still in good condition.

You decide to abandon it and escape so you enter a nearby cave entrance in the spider's den, the cave is too small for the spider to pursue.

Regardless, you stick with your choice to stay in the web and you slowly succumb to the injuries and die.

The Mole Village

An unknown smell wakes you up and you find yourself in some soft material. While injured, you get up and decide you need to do something.

You have the sudden urge to sit, you feel very tired and exhausted. You sit down on the soft material you landed in and suddenly close your eyes to get some rest.

You awake and discover the once vibrant place full of life is gone and you are now in a barren wasteland. You continue to sit until you eventually die from lack of story progression.

You decided to explore your surroundings and that sitting around was a dumb idea.

You wander around and discover there is some sort of civilization underground as the villagers and other humanoid figures enter and exit an entrance.

You do not know if you should interact with those “people” or head to find some water in the forest.

You decided to think about your situation, you thought for a very good minute. Lost in thought, you think of what to do in this situation.

The more you thought, the more you were thinking. After all the thinking you decided moving around was your best option.

After determining a choice, you think how thinking was a useless option and should have just chosen to explore your surroundings.

You head into the forest , deciding that interacting with “people” is not worth it.

Thirsty and hungry, you seek to find a water source. You hear the rush of water and go straight towards it.

After finding the water source and taking a drink, you clean your wounds and wrap it with the leaves around you. You then think about what you should do to enact revenge upon your corrupt sister.

You decide to enter the village and try to seek help from the “people”. As you enter the village, many look at you with curiosity and awe.

As you limp, covered in blood, a traveler helps you and takes you to the local doctor. They help you stitch your wounds and give you some food to eat.

All types of communication do not work due to the language barrier between each other.

Although there is a language barrier, you still want to say thanks to “people” that helped you. How will you thank them?

You thank them for their kindness even though there is a language barrier. They do not understand what you were saying but they knew it was meant with good intentions.

WIth that, they also give you a comically large spoon as a gift to help you on your way, you thank them and decide it's time to take revenge on your sister. You leave and start your long journey.

You dismiss their kindness and demand more from them. They do not understand what you have said, however they do understand that you do not have good intentions.

The mole villagers start making an ear piercing noise and rush towards you…

You stay silent and they stare in confusion. You start to exit the hut but although confused, they give you a comically large spoon as a gift to help you on your way.

You stare at them with no emotion, grab the spoon and leave. As you think about how to take revenge on your sister, the people just stare in confusion on what just happened.

You decided that it was best to do nothing. You did nothing to patch your wounds, find food or water, or do anything of that matter.

You just decided to do absolutely nothing. Since you did nothing, you slowly succumbed to the injuries and died.

The Sea Shore

You awake in salty water with sand on your face. Dazed and injured, you start to walk and limp away from the cold salty water and rest under the shade of a palm tree.

You decided you need to move and figure out a way to secure food, water, and to patch your wounds.

You head into the forest thinking that's the most logical thing to do. With thirst and hunger on your mind, you seek the forest for a water source.

You hear the rush of water and go straight towards it. After finding the water source and taking a drink, you clean your wounds and wrap it with the leaves around you.

You then think about what you should do to enact revenge upon your corrupt sister.

You decided swimming was your best option. You head to the water and start swimming along the shore. However, the blood from the injuries attracted sharks and they were hungry for food.

You adventure off into the unknown world, passing many landmarks on the way. After many nights in the forest, you stumble upon a village full of people.

You try to find a building to stay in but the language barrier and the lack of money make people drive you away.

You adapt quickly and try to figure out how to learn the language of the people and find money. You look at loose papers and listen to locals reading it aloud to others.

After days of learning and sleeping back in the forest, you are now able to speak bits of the language.

With that out the way, you need to find money and grow stronger to defeat your sister. You see a bounty board and decide to gain strength and money there.

You decide to head to the graveyard. You follow the directions on the paper leading you to the destination.

When you arrive, you do not see the bounty at first. Only a column of pillars surrounds the concrete grave in the middle.

You notice shifting in one of the pillars, almost like it is breathing. It noticed that you caught on and revealed itself as the bounty.

The pillar held together by the unknown ghastly features of the bounty, it prepares to engage.

The once possessed pillar disappears and the pieces fall to the ground. With the bounty defeated, you take a piece of the broken pillar as proof.

Also the other pillars were illusions and they fade away as the possessed pillar is defeated. You head back to the village and the hut next to the board awards you with 10 gold coins.

With money taken care of, you still need more experience before you feel comfortable enough to defeat your sister.

You also slowly feel your wings grow stronger though you are not sure. Without wondering more, you decided to take on another quest.

You decide to head to the ruined temple. You follow the directions on the paper leading you to the destination.

When you arrive, all you see around you is overly grown greenery that covers the temple walls. As you're about to enter the ruins, you hear the dragging of a sharp object coming through all the entrances.

The presence of the bounty approaching does not phase you, you keep your calm and watch the entrances.

As it approaches, you spot its features, a dark midnight color armour set along with a long black cape reaching his reinforced boots. His giant greatspear creates sparks on the ground as he walks.

You prepare yourself for the battle ahead.

The dark knight kneels and slowly fades away, leaving behind the armour and weapon. The armour and weapon are not in a shape where you can use for yourself.

You grab the nearly broken helmet to take as proof. You head back to the village and the hut next to the board awards you with 10 gold coins.

With money taken care of, you still need more experience before you feel comfortable enough to defeat your sister.

You also feel your wings grow stronger. Although they are not back to their original strength.

It is more than noticable about the physical improvements you have gained. Without wondering more, you decided to take on the last quest.

You decide to head to the bar. You follow the directions on the paper leading you to the destination. When you arrive, the entrance of the bar looks run down.

You enter and spot what seems to be a warrior who lays unconscious. As you move closer, the warrior awakes and spots you, he speaks in mumbles and broken language, however you do not understand him.

The warriors' worn-out silver colored armour appears to have some sort of glow to them. The warrior stumbles to get up but you dare not lower your guard.

You spot the warriors glowing dual-greatswords as they go towards them. They speak to you, however you only know so much. As they face you, the only thing you understand is that they said “Prepare”.

The warrior, unable to accept defeat, goes into a flurry of rage. However, before they can do that they ultimately succumb to the injuries and falls.

The warrior becomes dust and the armour and swords lose their glow. They are now worthless equipment.

However, you grab the swords as proof and head out of the bar. You head back to the village and the hut next to the board awards you with 10 gold coins.

With that, you feel that the amount of experience you had gained is enough to overpower your sisters corrupt ways. You can feel that your wings had gotten significantly stronger, even before you started your journey here.

After defeating all bounties, you stay in the village for a while and learn the language completely.

With your stay, you also gain some companions. You tell your companions your story and how your sister has done those corrupt deeds.

With companions backing you up, and your wings strong enough are able to now fly. However you need more power and speed to reach the kingdom far in the clouds.

You come up with a plan with your companions to take revenge on your sister. You remember that the kingdom does not stay in the same spot, however you do know it follows a fixed path throughout the year.

You and your companions determine that the kingdom will be above a mountain famous for its strong winds. Which would be a perfect place and time to go back to the castle.
