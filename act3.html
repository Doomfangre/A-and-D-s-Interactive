Hymn of Daedalus

You and your companions determine the kingdom should be in the general area of the mountain in a couple of days. The mountain is shaped like a volcano, however there is no lava present due to the irregularities of the mountain.

The plan was to create a funnel big enough to where it directs the wind into the bottom of the mountain. In response to that, it shoots all the air out of the top kinda like an air volcano.

So, you and your companions head off towards the mountains after getting the necessary materials for the plan to work. After reaching the destination, you and your companions start the plan.

You get there before the allotted time when the kingdom would be there to have the necessary time to commence the plan. After many hardships from the harsh winds messing up the plan, the funnel is completed.

You spend the remainder of your time with your companions before your time is up. When the allotted time came, you looked at your companions who were with you throughout your journey.

Leave with Silence

You look at your companions and wave goodbye to them. They do the same and wish you luck on the rest of your journey.

You walk back to your companions and give them a proper goodbye instead of a wave. You then go back to the cave entrance and spread your wings to catch yourself with the wind and launch out of the mountain up into the sky.

You fly around a bit and discover the kingdom. You start to fly towards it.

You looked at your companions and remembered the great times with them. Mixed emotions flow into you at once.

You feel like you do not want this feeling of companionship to end. You fall into a deep thought, like time has stopped for that instant.

After what felt like forever, you decide that your family is now your companions and that revenge is something you do not truly want anymore.

You decide to stay and rush towards your companions explaining your feelings. They accept your feelings and you leave the mountain. Losing your change in revenge.

You live life with your companions adventuring and exploring this vast world together.

Stay Ending

You look at them for the last time, without exchanging any words. No goodbyes, no happy smiles, nothing.

You look at them and then towards the sky. Remembering your plan you spread your wings and leave. You get launched out of the mountain up into the sky.

You fly around a bit and discover the kingdom. You start to fly towards it, however with deep regret as you did not say goodbye to your companions. Regretting how it is now too late.

After this long journey you finally make it back to the kingdom. With revenge in mind you sneak into the castle through the many hidden passageways that your father had shown you in the past.

You cover yourself and your face with a long piece of cloth. Then you swiftly pass the multiple guards in the kingdom eventually reaching the throne room.

You are spotted by your sister and the guards. After asking who you are and a bit of rage baiting your sister, you reveal yourself.

Relatives, maids, butlers, royal guards, and even your corrupt younger sister are shocked to find you still alive. Angered, you reveal everything that your sister has said and done.

With everyone in shock, your sister wont allow you to take what she has, so she uses her crown to control the royal guards. You see that the crown suddenly starts to glow and the guard's behavior has changed.

Overwhelmed, you are unable to fight back and are struck down by the guards. Heavily injured, you remember the technique that you had accumulated during your journey down below.

You release your aura and the guards are blown away. Your sister, who is not ready to go down without a fight, decides to duel you.

However, before she can start, you knock her down and grab the crown. You toss it to the floor and crush it with a heavy stomp. The guards come back to their senses and you look at your sister with hatred.

You decided to end her life, to get the revenge you always wanted. You raise your sword and cut her down.

Everyone, in shock, understood what you had done, although they really wished you had not done that. You reveal yourself to the public, explaining your situation, and how your sister has been defeated.

The public, confused but happy of your return, applause and you and thus ending your sisters tyranny. Not long after your return, you officially become the rightful ruler of the kingdom.

With your younger sister buried in the cloud cemetery of corrupt rulers, you hold her head high, thinking this was the best thing to do. You slowly become used to the style of living and after a couple of years, you are now a family of four.

You had an arranged marriage with a princess from another kingdom in the sky. You had one son and one daughter.

Your daughter suddenly goes missing and for a week, you relentlessly search for her. You ultimately find her again in okay shape and quickly help her get the medical attention she needs.

For the next couple of days you notice a change in her behavior, but you brush it off. Forgetting what happened to you in the beginning.

You sleep one day and never wake up after drinking a glass of night-time water given by your daughter. The cycle continues…

Bad Ending

You decided to end her life, to get the revenge you always wanted. You raise your sword and prepare to cut her down. You look at your younger sister who you used to have a great close bond with.

The thought of killing your sister overwhelms you and you drop the sword. Your sister immediately tries to retaliate, however is overwhelmed by your brute strength. You detain her and she is placed under arrest for her crimes.

You reveal yourself to the public, explaining the evil deeds that your sister has done and how she is now detained. The public, happy that your sisters tyranny is over, praises you and applauds you and welcomes you back.

You slowly get adjusted with your royalty status and your relatives and royal guards are proud of the decision you made. They lead you to the statue of your mother and father, the statues seem to glow without reason.

Confused, you brush it off to be the sunlight shining upon the statues. After years of getting adjusted and your sister spending her remaining years in prison.

You are now a family of four, as you had an arranged marriage with a princess from another kingdom in the sky. You had one son and one daughter.

One day, your daughter goes missing, however she was quickly found as your younger sister had alerted the guards. The mastermind was a criminal that was sought out for years, ever since your father was king.

Your sister explains how she was brainwashed and abused by this mastermind. Not wanting your daughter to go through what she did, she alerted the guards once she heard a sneaking intruder.

After this incident, you live on to be one of the greatest kings to ever rule the kingdom.
